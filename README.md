# Life in weeks

A thoughtful, minimalist web app that visualizes your life in weeks. Enter your birth date to see your life laid out as a grid of weeks, along with meaningful statistics about time.

## Features

- **2-step flow**: Select your birth date via calendar input, then view your life visualization
- **Interactive weeks grid**: Visual representation of 75 years (3,910 weeks) as a grid
  - Black squares: weeks you've lived
  - Grey square with border: your current week
  - Light grey squares: weeks ahead of you
- **Life statistics**:
  - Weeks lived and weeks remaining
  - Percentage of life lived
  - Seasons lived (6 per year in Bangladesh)
  - Weeks spent sleeping (8 hours per day)
  - Days spent sleeping
- **Fully responsive**: Works beautifully on mobile, tablet, and desktop
- **Minimalist design**: Inspired by Dieter Rams' design philosophy
- **Sentence case**: Throughout the entire interface

## Design Philosophy

This app is designed to be a moment of reflection. It encourages you to pause and think about the passage of time in a meaningful way. The minimalist design removes distractions and focuses on what matters: your life, visualized.

## Technical Details

- Pure vanilla JavaScript (no frameworks)
- CSS Grid for responsive layouts
- Average lifespan: 75 years (based on Bangladesh average of 74.67 years)
- Weeks per year: 52.14 weeks

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Click the calendar icon or date input field
3. Select your birth date
4. Click "View my life"
5. Explore your life visualization and statistics
6. Click "Calculate another life" to start over

- `styles.css` - All styling and responsive design
